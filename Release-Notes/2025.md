## Date 05/08/2025  
## Release 1.2508.0.0

### POS

#### [FEATURES]
**Brand Separation Logic**
- Introduced two distinct levels of brand association:
  - **Store Brands**: Each store can be linked to a brand. This association controls access to contacts, products, vouchers, promotions, and fidelity cards. Only brand-relevant data is visible and usable in each store.
  - **Product Brands (ProducerLines)**: Product brands can be associated with specific stores. A store will only see, sell, and interact with products whose brands have been explicitly assigned to it.

#### [IMPROVEMENTS]
- Refactored fidelity point calculation logic for improved accuracy and maintainability.
- Refactored verified return flow to ensure stability and consistent behavior across edge cases.
- Improved handling of multi-quantity promotions to ensure correct application across all eligible items and quantities.

#### [FIXES]
- Fixed an issue where free gifts were not correctly applied in promotional scenarios.
